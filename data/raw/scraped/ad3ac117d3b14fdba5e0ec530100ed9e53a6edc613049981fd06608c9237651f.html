Our Story

What has grown to become Danpong Healthcare

What has grown to become Danpong Healthcare started as Danpong Pharmacy (GH) Ltd in September 1989, when Dr Yaw Adu Gyamfi (Dr Danpong) formed his initial pharmacy supply company. He launched his first pharmacy shop at Nungua in 1991 with only eight workers, toiling from 7 am to 11 pm and often after hours to build a community pharmacy that is loved by the people of Nungua.
