About TTH

The Tamale Teaching Hospital (TTH) was commissioned in 1974 as a Regional Hospital to provide health care services to the people of Tamale and its environs. It was also to serve as the main medical referral center for the Northern Sector of the country including Upper East and West regions. The Hospital previously operated from the premises of the Old Central Hospital and later moved to its present location after the construction of the new Hospital was completed and commissioned in 1974.

Learn More

Tertiary Health Care

The Hospital has since 2009 been upgraded to a Teaching Hospital to serve as the only tertiary health provider in the whole of the Savanna regions including the Upper West and East regions, Northern Region, some parts of the Brong–Ahafo region and the Northern parts of the Volta region. The Hospital also receives clients from some parts of Burkina Faso and Togo

Learn More

Medical Education

The Hospital currently has a bed capacity of eight hundred and twelve (812) beds. The Hospital provides its services to over hundred thousand patients every year. TTH is located in the eastern part of the Tamale Metropolis, on the main Tamale-Salaga Road and has a land area of about four hundred and ninety thousand (490,000m2) square meters.

CEO's Message

I extend warm greetings from the Tamale Teaching Hospital (TTH). This is Northern Ghana's premier referral health facility. The hospital has been in operation for 50 years, serving thousands of patients from Ghana and other West African countries. We have also had a number of high-profile clients from southern Ghana and other countries who came to Ghana and needed urgent medical care, which we provided with distinction. We have a team of exceptional and highly motivated employees who are prepared to provide you with services. The hospital is strategically placed to provide tertiary health care to clients, promote and conduct scientific research and training of health professionals for Ghana and beyond. We are among the first health institutions in Ghana to try to employ IT solutions to help solve health care challenges, such as reducing patient waiting times, assessing staff productivity, and improving data collection for management decisions and scientific research. I cordially invite you to the Tamale Teaching Hospital, where you will be treated by renowned Consultants and highly motivated allied health professionals such as Laboratory Scientists, Nurses, Radiographers, and others. Visit the Tamale Teaching Hospital to give your health the boost it needs. We aim for nothing less than excellence. I hope to see you at the hospital.

Mental Health

Mental health is a critical aspect of our overall well-being, influencing how we think, feel, and act in daily life. It encompasses our emotional, psychological, and social well-being, affecting how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Our mental health services aim to support individuals in achieving and maintaining their best mental state, offering a safe and confidential environment to explore personal challenges. We provide a range of services, including individual counseling, group therapy sessions, and workshops on stress management, mindfulness, and emotional resilience. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping you navigate life's difficulties, fostering a sense of balance and well-being in your life

Public Health

Our public health services are designed to address the needs of communities, ranging from health promotion and disease prevention to emergency preparedness and response. We work on initiatives such as vaccination programs, health education campaigns, nutritional guidance, and environmental health efforts to ensure safe and healthy living conditions for all. Our team collaborates with local, national, and international organizations to monitor health trends, identify health risks, and implement strategies that minimize health disparities

Palliative Care

Our team of healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, social workers, and chaplains, work together to provide a support system for the patient and their family, helping them navigate through complex healthcare decisions and providing care that aligns with the patient's goals and values.

Physiotherapy

Our physiotherapy services aim to help individuals recover from injuries, reduce pain, avoid surgery, manage chronic illnesses, and improve overall physical health and well-being. Through a combination of manual therapy, exercises, and the application of modalities like heat, ice, and electrical stimulation, we tailor our approach to meet the specific needs of each patient.
